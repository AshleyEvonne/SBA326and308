Can I Pet That Dogg?

The diversity of dog breeds worldwide is a testament to both human ingenuity and the adaptability of canines. Dogs have been selectively bred for centuries to fulfill various roles, from companionship to hunting, herding, guarding, and more. This selective breeding has led to a staggering array of breeds, each with unique physical and behavioral traits suited to different tasks and environments.
